Fried Rice Recipe

Description

A fast, tasty dish made with leftover rice, veggies, and eggs. Perfect for a weeknight meal or a quick lunch fix!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, leftover rice)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions(for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Scramble eggs: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, scramble eggs, set aside
  2. Stir-fry veggies: In the same pan, add more oil, saute garlic and onions. Add veggies and cook until just tender.
  3. Add rice & sauces: Add rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Combine: Add scrambled eggs back in. Toss everything together.
  5. Garnish & serve: Top with chopped spring onions. Done!
