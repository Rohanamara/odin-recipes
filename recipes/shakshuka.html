Shakshuka Recipe

Description

A flavorful Middle Eastern dish of poached eggs in spiced tomato and pepper sauce. Great for breakfast, bruch or even dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3-4 eggs
  • Fresh coriander or parsley(for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Saute veggies: Heat oil in a pan. Cook onions and peppers until soft. Add garlic and cook for a minute.
  2. Add spices & tomatoes: Stir in paprika, cumin, chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Add tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  3. Add eggs: Make small wells in the sauce, crack in the eggs. Cover and cook on low for 5-7 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  4. Serve: Garnish with herbs. Serve hot with bread or pita!
